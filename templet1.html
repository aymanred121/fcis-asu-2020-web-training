Musacaa

Musacaa is Egyptian dish made out of mainly eggplant and other vegetable. It is good to eat with some pita bread


how to make Musacaa

Ingredients:


      2 big egg plant
      1 Bell pepper
      2 big onion (1 is grated and the other one is sliced)
      2 cloves garlic
      1 cup Tomato paste
      ¼ ground beef
      1 tsp Baharat
      1 tsp pepper
      1 tsp paprika
      to taste Salt

Directions


1 - Cut the bellpepper,tomato, pepper and onion. And in the other plate separate the onion and 2 cloves of garlic cut into small pieces for saute.

2 - Preheat the oven Into 190 degree and bake the eggplant and pepper sprinkle some olive oil.

3 - In a small pan in a medium heat, sauté garlic and onion bell pepper and when its sight brown add the beef. Cook for 10 minutes and add the tomato sauce and cut tomato and onion boil for 5 minutes.

4 - After the sauté beef is cooked add the eggplant that you bake and the pepper. Boil them for another 5 minutes all together then it was ready to serve.
